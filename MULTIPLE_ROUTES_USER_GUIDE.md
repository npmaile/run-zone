### Step 1: Generate Route Options

1. **Set your distance** using the bottom panel
2. **Look for the green map icon** (ğŸ—ºï¸) at the top
3. **Tap the icon** to generate options
4. **Wait a moment** while 4 routes are created

### Step 2: Browse Your Options

The route selection sheet shows:
- **4 route cards** with different styles
- **Distance** for each option
- **Waypoint count** (how many turns)

1. **Tap any route card** to select it
2. **See it highlighted** in blue with a checkmark

### Step 4: Start Running!

- Your selected route is now active
- Directional arrows show the way
- Voice navigation guides you
- Start your run!

### Understanding the Stats

**Distance**:
- Shows actual calculated distance
- May vary slightly from target
- All options close to your goal

**Waypoints**:
- More waypoints = more turns
- Fewer waypoints = straighter route
- Excludes start and end points

**Complexity**:
- **Low**: Simple route, few turns
- **Medium**: Moderate variety
- **High**: Many turns, complex path

## ğŸ¤” Common Questions

### Q: Can I generate new options?
**A**: Yes! Just tap the map icon again to generate 4 new routes.

### Q: Will the routes be different each time?
**A**: Yes! The algorithm adds randomness, so you'll get fresh routes every time.

### Q: Which style is best for beginners?
**A**: Start with **Balanced** - it's designed for everyday runs.

### Q: Can I change after starting?
**A**: You need to stop your run first, then generate new options.

### Q: What if I don't like any of them?
**A**: Tap the map icon again to generate 4 completely new routes!

### Q: Do they all have the same distance?
**A**: They're all close to your target, but may vary slightly (usually within 0.5km).

### Q: Why choose Varied over Scenic?
**A**: **Varied** is even more exploratory than Scenic, with maximum unpredictability!
